Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-04 · Effective 2026-06-04

This Privacy Policy describes how the TrustStars iOS app and the TrustStars Chrome extension ("TrustStars", "we") handle information. TrustStars helps you judge the star rating you can actually trust by applying the Wilson confidence score to a product's average rating and number of reviews.

In short: TrustStars collects no data about you, contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking, and sends nothing to us or any third party. Everything stays on your device.

What we do not collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, address, or any contact info.
  • Your location.
  • Your photos, contacts, microphone, camera, or other device data.
  • Advertising identifiers, device fingerprints, or any tracking identifiers.
  • Usage, browsing, or interaction analytics. There is no analytics SDK, crash-reporting SDK, advertising SDK, or marketing SDK of any kind.

The iOS app

The app stores the products you add to compare (a name you type, the average stars, and the review count) on your device only, via Apple's standard UserDefaults. It makes no network connections. This data is removed when you delete the app.

The Chrome extension

The extension reads the average rating and review-count numbers that are already displayed on a product page you are viewing (for example, an Amazon product page) in order to calculate and show the confidence-adjusted "trusted score." That reading happens entirely within your browser. The comparison list you build is stored locally on your device via chrome.storage.local. The extension makes no network requests and transmits nothing to us or anyone else.

Browser permissions

  • activeTab / scripting — to read the rating shown on the current product page when you click "Scan this page."
  • storage — to save your comparison list locally on your device.
  • Host access (e.g. amazon.*) — to read the visible rating and review count on supported product pages so the badge can be shown.

These permissions are used solely to read publicly displayed rating numbers and to store your list locally. No page content or personal information is collected or sent anywhere.

Third parties

TrustStars integrates no third-party services, SDKs, or trackers. We do not share, sell, or rent any information — because there is nothing to share.

Children

TrustStars is a general-audience tool. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Your rights

Because TrustStars collects no personal information, there is no record for us to access, correct, or delete. To remove everything stored on your device, delete the app or remove the extension from your browser.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
