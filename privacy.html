Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-06-14 · Effective 2026-06-14

This Privacy Policy describes how the TrustStars iOS app and the TrustStars Chrome extension ("TrustStars", "we") handle information. TrustStars helps you judge the star rating you can actually trust by applying the Wilson confidence score to a product's average rating and number of reviews.

In short: TrustStars does its work entirely on your device, contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking, and does not monitor your browsing. The only information that ever leaves your device is a site suggestion you choose to submit using the optional "Suggest a site" form.

What we do not collect

  • Your browsing history, the pages you visit, or the products you view.
  • Your location.
  • Your photos, contacts, microphone, camera, or other device data.
  • Advertising identifiers, device fingerprints, or any tracking identifiers.
  • Usage, browsing, or interaction analytics. There is no analytics SDK, crash-reporting SDK, advertising SDK, or marketing SDK of any kind.

We do not collect your email or any contact information unless you voluntarily type it into the "Suggest a site" form and submit it (see below).

The iOS app

The app stores the products you add to compare (a name you type, the average stars, and the review count) on your device only, via Apple's standard UserDefaults. It makes no network connections. This data is removed when you delete the app.

The Chrome extension

The extension reads the average rating and review-count numbers that are already displayed on a product page you are viewing (for example, an Amazon product page) in order to calculate and show the confidence-adjusted "trusted score." That reading happens entirely within your browser and is never transmitted anywhere. The comparison list you build, your settings, and the "wrong prediction" memory (a small list of page patterns you've marked as incorrect) are all stored locally on your device via chrome.storage.local and are never sent to us.

"Suggest a site" (the only data we receive)

The extension includes an optional form for suggesting a store you'd like supported. If — and only if — you fill it in and press send, the extension transmits the information you entered to our server at api.pythonenthusiast.com:

  • the site URL you suggest;
  • your email address (so we can follow up);
  • an optional note you write.

We use this solely to evaluate and add new sites. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for marketing, and our server records the submission's network (IP) address only to enforce a basic anti-abuse limit (a maximum of 10 suggestions per day). You can ask us to delete a suggestion you sent by emailing the address below. If you never use the form, nothing is sent.

Browser permissions

  • activeTab / scripting — to read the rating shown on the current product page when you click "Scan this page."
  • storage — to save your comparison list, settings, and prediction memory locally on your device.
  • Host access to supported stores (e.g. amazon.*) — to read the visible rating and review count on supported product pages so the badge can be shown.
  • Host access to api.pythonenthusiast.com — used only to deliver a "Suggest a site" form submission when you send one.

These permissions are used solely to read publicly displayed rating numbers, store your data locally, and deliver suggestions you choose to send. No page content is collected or transmitted.

Third parties

TrustStars integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Site suggestions you submit are received by our own server and are not shared, sold, or rented to anyone.

Children

TrustStars is a general-audience tool. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your rights

Other than a "Suggest a site" submission you choose to send, TrustStars keeps no personal information about you. To remove everything stored on your device, delete the app or remove the extension from your browser. To request deletion of a suggestion you submitted, email us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
